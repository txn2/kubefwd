# Code of Conduct

## Principle

Treat everyone with respect. We're here to write good software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be professional and courteous
- Focus on the technical merits of contributions
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Disagree respectfully when you disagree

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment of any kind
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Conduct inappropriate for a professional setting

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to Craig Johnston <cj@imti.co>. Reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately. Maintainers may remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code, and may ban repeat offenders.

## Scope

This code applies to project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) and when representing the project publicly.
